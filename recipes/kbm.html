KBM

(short for 'Kefta, Bid w Maticha' which translates to meatballs, egg and tomato)

Overview

KBM is a popular tajine dish in Morocco, and undoubtedly one of the fastest and simplest dishes to make!

Ingredients

(for one serving)

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100g meatballs
  • Spices(1/2 tsp each):
    • salt
    • cumin
    • paprika
    • black pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Peel and grate the onion
  2. Peel and dice the tomato
  3. Add into a tajine or a pan
  4. Add the garlic, spices, and a dash of olive oil
  5. Cook on low heat for 10 mins while stirring
  6. Add meatballs
  7. After meatballs are coorked, add the egg and cover with a lid leaving it to cook for 5 mins
  8. Serve with bread as a side, and enjoy!
